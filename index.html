Regarding Miss Marcéy -

From the limited information we’ve obtained, we find Miss Aline Marcéy to be a very pressing
matter. Though she seems to be a new threat, she’s been causing chaos for quite some time
under the alias Liane Céymar. She’s been spotted at several crime scenes but the FBI has yet
to catch her. We’ve found Marcéy guilty of several murders from the past, all of which, have
been under the orders of someone else. Based on this, we can assume her “profession” is
assassination. After talking with some of her past “clients” we’ve discovered that she has been
running this sort of business for nearly ten years without the government's knowledge, it’s also
been discovered that she’s paid quite a large sum of money in return. in fact her current salary
is about three times larger than high ranking congressmen or other political figures of our nation.
Despite her major felonies that could easily qualify her for the death sentence the Government
has chosen to drop these charges, with ONE CONDITION: from here on out, Marcéy will work
for and only for the government, carrying out whichever given task she is assigned, regardless
of what that assignment may be. Failure to comply will result in her immediate execution. We
believe having Marcéy on our side will prove to be a very useful asset.

However, we acknowledge that there are many problems in this proposal…

  • We are unaware of Marcéy’s current whereabouts or general location.
  • There’s no telling whether or not she will comply to our agreements.
  • It’s possible she may take our offer with ulterior motives in mind.

To solve this problem we have chosen to send out our chosen agents in the hopes of locating Marcéy, the plan goes as follows:

  1. Using the given information, track down her main clientele with the hope of finding where her next hit will be.
  2. After confirming where and when her next hit will be, send out secret agents to secure target
  3. Once captured, bring Marcéy back to the base* where proposal will be introduced.

*depending on where her next hit is, base location will be determined sometime in the future.
